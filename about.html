About Focus Foods Hub

Our Story

Founded in 2024 by four Data Science students from UCP Lahore, Focus Foods Hub was born from a simple realization: eating healthy in Pakistan shouldn't be so complicated.

As students ourselves, we struggled to find nutritious food options that matched our fitness goals and budgets. The existing food delivery apps lacked nutritional transparency, and healthy eating felt like solving a complex puzzle every day.

We decided to build the solution ourselves - a platform that makes healthy eating simple, accessible, and personalized for everyone in Pakistan.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals in Pakistan to achieve their health and wellness goals by making personalized, nutritious eating simple, accessible, and sustainable.

Our Vision

To become Pakistan's most trusted health-focused food platform, helping millions make better food choices and building a healthier nation.
